Tomato and Eggs

Description

This dish is considered to be Chinese comfort food and simply made with 2-3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium tomatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 scallions
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp water

Steps

  1. Poach tomatoes in boiling water for 2 minutes, then let them cool and cut into 2 cm thick wedges.
  2. Finely chop scallions and set aside.
  3. In a medium-sized bowl, add eggs and 1 tbsp water. Lightly beat eggs, set aside.
  4. Turn up heat to medium high, hight 1 tbsp oil in a wok, and fry the eggs, stirring constantly to keep eggs in bite sized clumps. Remove eggs and set aside.
  5. Heat 1 more tbsp of oil and fry the scallions and tomatoes together until tomatoes are soft. Add salt and sugar. When water is released from tomatoes, mix in the eggs.
  6. Serve.
