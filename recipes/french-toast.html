French Toast

Description

Delicious french toast, soft and cinammony.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Beat together egg, vanilla, and cinnamon in a shallow dish.
  2. Stir in milk.
  3. Dip bread in the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides.
  4. Cook bread slices in a lightly greased pan until brown on both sides.
  5. Add your toppings and serve.
